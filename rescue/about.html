About Us

wild rescues is a registered nonprofit wildlife rescue and rehabilitation facility , located in ocala, florida. we help injured, sick neglected and orphaned wildlife. we help rehabilitate these animals and then release them back into the wild, of possible.

Wild life rescues is a member of the national wildlife rehabilitators association

We help many animals such as:

  • racoons
  • Squirrels
  • Fox
  • Birds
  • Horses
  • Deers
  • Pigs
  • reptiles

Did you find an injured or orphaned animal? Contact us to see if we can help.
